Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Focus Timer Pro respects your privacy.

We believe your data belongs to you, and you alone.

Our Privacy Commitment

  • No ads — We don't show advertisements or track you for advertising purposes
  • No analytics — We don't collect usage statistics or behavioral data
  • No tracking — We don't use cookies, pixels, or any tracking technologies
  • No data leaves your device — All your data stays on your local browser
  • No account required — No sign-ups, no emails, no personal information needed
  • No third-party services — We don't send data to external servers or services

What Data Is Stored

Focus Timer Pro stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's storage API:

  • Timer settings and session history
  • Blocked website patterns and schedules
  • Your PIN (if you enable PIN protection)
  • Extension preferences and settings

Data Storage Location

All data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not synced to any cloud service
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser data

Permissions Explained

Focus Timer Pro requests the following permissions:

  • Storage — To save your settings and timer data locally
  • Alarms — To trigger timer notifications and scheduled blocks
  • Notifications — To alert you when timers complete
  • Tabs & Scripting — To display block overlays on websites you choose to block
  • All URLs — Required to block any website you specify (we only act on sites you configure)

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in new versions of the extension. We will never reduce privacy protections without clear notice.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please reach out through the developer's page.
